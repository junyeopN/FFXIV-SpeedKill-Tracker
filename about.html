There are two modes in FFXIV Speedkill Tracker: HPTracker and SpeedTracker

1. HPTracker

If the raid has one main boss, we have to
measure how much hp the boss has at
the end of each phase.

2. SpeedTracker

If the raid has different bosses in each phases,
we have to measure the time that each phase
ended in.
